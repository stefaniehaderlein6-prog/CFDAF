<section id="who-we-support">
  <h2>Who We Support</h2>
  <p>
    We provide direct financial assistance to individuals facing genuine hardship, including the disabled,
    unemployed, students, workers, retired individuals, low-income earners, young adults, and older citizens
    across the USA, UK, and Canada.
  </p>
</section>

<hr>

<section id="eligibility-requirements">
  <h2>Eligibility Requirements</h2>
  <p>To qualify for CFDAF support, you must:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Be 18 years or older</li>
    <li>Live in the USA, UK, or Canada</li>
    <li>Be experiencing financial hardship</li>
    <li>Fall into at least one support category: Disabled, Unemployed, Student, Worker, Retired, Low-Income Earner</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<hr>

<section id="what-you-need">
  <h2>What You’ll Need</h2>
  <p>Please prepare the following for your application:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Valid government-issued ID</li>
    <li>Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, etc.)</li>
    <li>Recent photo</li>
    <li>Brief explanation of your current financial situation</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<hr>

<section id="how-it-works">
  <h2>How the Process Works</h2>
  <p>Our process is simple, safe, and transparent:</p>
  <ol>
    <li>Check Eligibility</li>
    <li>Complete Application Form</li>
    <li>Submit Documents</li>
    <li>Verification Call</li>
    <li>Secure Verification (Fee / Micro-Deposit / KYC)</li>
    <li>Approval Notification</li>
    <li>Cash Disbursement</li>
    <li>Confirmation & Receipt</li>
  </ol>
</section>

<hr>

<section id="verification-security">
  <h2>Verification & Security</h2>
  <p>
    To protect applicants and CFDAF from fraud, a secure identity or bank verification step is required before
    funds are released. This may include:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>A small refundable Admin Verification Fee</li>
    <li>Bank micro-deposit verification (no fee)</li>
    <li>KYC identity check</li>
    <li>Fee waiver for applicants who cannot pay</li>
  </ul>
  <p>This ensures funds go to the rightful person safely.</p>
</section>

<hr>

<section id="admin-verification-fee">
  <h2>Admin Verification Fee</h2>
  <p>
    In some cases, applicants may be asked to complete a small refundable Admin Verification Fee to finalize
    identity and payment verification.
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>Fully refundable at disbursement or if the application is denied</li>
    <li>Low-cost ($5–$20 or local equivalent)</li>
    <li>Fee waivers available for those unable to pay</li>
    <li>Secure payments only (no gift cards, crypto, or cash apps)</li>
  </ul>
  <p><strong>➤ CFDAF will never ask for gift cards or codes.</strong></p>
</section>

<hr>

<section id="benefit-amounts">
  <h2>Benefit Amounts</h2>
  <p>Support amounts vary based on need, category, household size, and available funding:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Emergency Relief: $100 – $1,000</li>
    <li>Family / Household Support: $250 – $2,000</li>
    <li>Students: $100 – $600</li>
    <li>Persons with Disabilities: $300 – $1,500</li>
    <li>Retirees / Elderly Support: $100 – $1,000</li>
    <li>Unemployment Transition Support: $150 – $1,200</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<hr>

<section id="after-you-apply">
  <h2>After You Apply</h2>
  <p>Once your application is submitted:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>You will receive a “Pending Review” notification</li>
    <li>A CFDAF team member will contact you for a verification call</li>
    <li>If approved, you will receive a “Final Approval” message and disbursement details</li>
    <li>Cash support will be released directly to you</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<hr>

<section id="pending-verification">
  <h2>Pending Verification Notice</h2>
  <h3>Your Application is Pending Verification</h3>
  <p>
    Your application has been received. Before funds are released, we must complete a secure identity and payment
    verification step to protect your safety. You may be asked to:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>Complete a short verification call</li>
    <li>Confirm bank account ownership via micro-deposit</li>
    <li>Pay a small refundable Admin Verification Fee, or request a fee waiver</li>
  </ul>
  <p>
    Once verification is completed and approved, your funds will be released in cash.
    <br><br>
    <strong>CFDAF will NEVER request gift cards, codes, or cryptocurrency to activate or release funds.</strong>
  </p>
</section>

<hr>

<section id="anti-scam-warning">
  <h2>Anti-Scam Warning</h2>
  <p><strong>For your protection:</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li>✅ We do not require gift cards of any kind</li>
    <li>✅ We do not charge application or processing fees</li>
    <li>❗ Any person asking for gift cards, codes, or “clearance fees” is a scammer</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Report suspicious activity to CFDAF immediately.</p>
</section>
